Explore Somerset West

Things To Do

Vergelegen Wine Estate

Vergelegen was founded in 1700 with award-winning wines spanning for over 300 years. They have picturesque gardens, comprehensive cuisine options to suit all tastes and a variety of experiences such as wine tasting, heritage, environmental and cellar tours.

Read More

Lourensford Wine Estate

The estate was established in 1709, surrounded by gardens, vineyards and the view of Helderberg.They offer a range of different experiences such as wine tasting, vineyard tours, vodka tasting and exploring loacal stalls at the Lourensford Market.

Read More

Helderberg Nature Reserve

Helderberg Nature Reserve is located on the slopes of the Helderberg Mountain. They offer several hiking trails ranging from 2.2km to 8.2km where you will be able to explore the fauna and flora. They also have a restaurant, picnic spots and regularly host events.

Read More

Cheetah Outreach

Cheetah Outreach has been promoting the survival of the free ranging South African cheetah for more than 20 years. They offer a variety of different activities for both children and adults. These include a cheetah encouter, walk with a Cheetah, Meerkat or Caracal. They also offer encouters with other widlife as well as a feeding and enrichment tour.

Read More

Blue Rock Adventure Park

Blue Rock is a water and adventure park located just outside of Somerset West. They offer watersport activities like waterskiing, wakeboarding, kneeboarding, canoeiing, rockjumping and ziplining. They also have an outdoor paintball field and a restaurant.

Read More

Strand Beach

The beach is a three-kilometre sandy stretch with a tidal pool which is safe for swimming. The beach also has a kiosk and boat launching site. Restaurants and shops are within walking distance as well a nearby fun-park that offers putt-putt and water slides.

Gordon's Bay Beach

It is a long sandy beach with shallow waters, which makes it safe for swimming. There are grass lawns and picnic tables, as well as restaurants, shops and cafes within walking distance.
