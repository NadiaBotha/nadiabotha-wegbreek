About Us

About The Location

Wegbreek Self Catering is located in a quiet neighboorhood in Somerset West, close to vineyards and parks with easy access to stores and other local attractions.

he property is only a 25-minite drive from Cape Town International Airport, making it easy for everyone to enjoy the breath taking views of Helderberg mountains.

Other than the city of Cape Town, Somerset West is also located near Stellenbosch, Strand and Gordon's Bay, enabling you to go wine tasting, swimming and eating fresh seafood, all within a 20-minute drive.

About The Guesthouse

Wegbreek Self Catering is a modern open-plan apartment which can comfortably accommodate 2 guests. The unit is located in a private residence, which is completely separate from the main house with a private entrance, parking and garden.

It has air-conditioning, a comfortable queen-size bed as well as a kitchen area, which has everything guests need for a care-free, self-catering stay. The kitchen includes a stove and oven, microwave, fridge, kettle as well as crockery and cutlery. The bathroom is wheelchair friendly with a shower, tiolet and basin.

Additional amenities include a study room, unlimeted wi-fi and a flat screen TV with access to some DSTV channels. This unit also has a fire oit and outdoor barbequeue facilities.
